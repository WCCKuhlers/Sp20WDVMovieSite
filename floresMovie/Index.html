The Breakfast Club

Directed by: John Hughes

Summary of Movie

Basically this movie about 5 teenagers who are completely different but similar walk into a saturday detention. They all have their own problems no matter how different they may be. It's the first time they met but it changed their lives forever. There's a brain, a beauty, a jock, a rebel, and a recluse. They come together and break rules but touch each other in ways they never thought they could. That's as much as I can really say without spoiling the movie to you.

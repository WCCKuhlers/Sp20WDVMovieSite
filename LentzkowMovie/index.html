Avengers: Endgame

Plot

Thanos, is trying to destroy the entire universe. Some Avengers have already died to thanos in Avengers infinity war.
The remaining Avengers reassemble to undo all the bad that thanos has caused. As they call in allies and travel back
and forth in time, they attempt to reverse the destruction of Thanos. They have to restore order to the universe.

Background

Thanos has been going around on planets killing half of their population in order to help the amount of food.
in order to make this process faster he needs to gather the 5 infity stones, but the avengers will do anything to stop him
